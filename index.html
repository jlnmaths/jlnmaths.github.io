Hi!

I am a fifth-year Economics PhD-student at Heidelberg University under supervision of Jörg Oechssler.

I completed the PhD-courses at Mannheim University and spent part of my fourth year visiting Ran Spiegler at University College London.

I write models and design experiments to address questions in behavioral economics and industrial organisation. I have also worked with field data from professional sports.

Contact me at julian.matthes [at] awi.uni-heidelberg.de.
